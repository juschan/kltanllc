About Us

KLTAN LLC was formed by incorporating the boutique law firm of K L Tan & Associates which was established in 1988. KLTAN LLC is a specialist law corporation focusing on Intellectual Property law and providing legal services in all areas of intellectual property and related transactional matters to both domestic and international clients.


KLTAN LLC aims to establish and build a solid and continuing relationship with all its clients by understanding their needs and providing prompt and efficient service at reasonable costs so that the clients’ rights are protected in the best possible way both locally and overseas. With its extensive global network of contacts, the company is able to assist the clients in protecting their intellectual property rights internationally. The company believes in providing value added service and advice that take into account the clients’ business goals and the importance of providing relevant and effective commercial advice on intellectual property matters.

Patents
  • Advising on patent requirements and protection
  • Patent filing & obtaining registration
  • Renewals & maintenance of grants

Designs

  • Conducting searches
  • Filing trade mark renewals and post-registration changes
  • Prosecuting and defending oppositions
  • Handling revocation actions
  • Advising on trade mark infringement and passing off actions
  • Licensing and Assignments
  • Confidentiality Agreements and Trade Secrets

Ang Su-Lin, Director
